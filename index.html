Interests and skills

Current interests? Programming of course! I have also always had a passion for music, whether it be playing, listening, or even producing. Solo performing was a challenge, but being part of these groups always made me strive to exceed my limitations. Some of my best relationships and experiences were produced through playing music. Another passion of mine has always been sports. I've never played competitively, only streetball with friends and family as a youth. However, if I do have time to watch television, its usually a basketball or football game. Sports news is also my first web browse every morning.
